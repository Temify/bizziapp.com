Intuitive solutions for sales

OVERVIEW 360

With the sophisticated 360 – a detailed overview of the client, company, and trade - you have all the information stored in an intelligible and compact way, and always at hand. From appointments, tasks and phases of the trade to the attached files and contracts, all is kept in one place. The entire card then offers the possibility of chronological ordering of all activities from the first contact through to the last phone call in the form of an intuitive timeline. All of this comes in a user-friendly form.

SPEED OF TASKING

We develop intelligent queues. We will adapt or recommend queues tailored to the pace of your company so you can use all the maximum possibilities of automation. Each task can be assigned with not only subtasks, files or comments, but just about anything necessary. Our task queues also respect the hierarchy of tasks - from the corporate, through teamwork, down to the individual.

GUIDE TO YOUR APPOINTMENTS

A vital tool, without which you will not want to step out of the office. A carefully prepared script of each meeting will ensure that nothing of importance is omitted. Complemented by multimedia attachments, it allows you to demonstrate the latest news to your clients, whether it be product documentation or video. All of this can be done remotely or in real time.

BIZZICALL

Linked with a private telecom solution, Bizziapp allows you to make and closely monitor all contact with your clients. Call details are automatically saved to the 360 and can later be put on the timeline. At the same time, we are able to evaluate the results of the call based on this data. Combining all these features creates a tool that will ensure your business has the highest level of customer support regardless of who is currently holding the handset.
